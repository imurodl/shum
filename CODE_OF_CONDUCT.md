# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, concise, and specific.

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differences.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on behavior and engineering outcomes, not individuals.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks and harassment.
- Disrespectful or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Threatening, abusive, or intimidating behavior.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues to project maintainers through the repository issue tracker.

## Scope

This policy applies to all project spaces, including pull requests, issues, and community forums.
